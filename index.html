PIXEL CAR SIM

Pixel-style physics demo — use ArrowUp to accelerate, ArrowDown to brake.

Instructions

Goal: drive along a 300 m track made of 3 surfaces: ICE, SAND, WOOD.

  • Ice — very low friction, slides easily.
  • Sand — high friction, slows you down.
  • Wood — medium friction.

Controls: ArrowUp = Accelerate, ArrowDown = Brake, Space = Pause/Resume, R = Restart.

Physics panel shows speed, position, acceleration, friction, slope.
